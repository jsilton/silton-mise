---
title: 'Cranberry Crunch'
ingredients:
  - '3 cups apples, peeled and sliced thin'
  - '2 cups cranberries, whole bag'
  - '3/4 cup sugar'
  - 'Topping'
  - '1 1/2 cup oatmeal'
  - '1/2 cup brown sugar'
  - '1/3 cup flour'
  - '1 stick marine, melted'
  - '1/3 cup chopped pecans, optional'
---

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven at 350 degrees.
2. Spray 9x13 dish with PAM.
3. Put apples and cranberries in bottom of dish.
4. Cover with sugar.
5. Mix topping together.
6. Spread thinly on top of dish.
7. Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour (done when bubbly).
