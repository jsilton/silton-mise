---
title: 'Nana''s Biscuits'
ingredients:
  - '¾ Cups buttermilk'
  - 'Pinch of Baking Soda'
  - '2 cups Self Rising Flour (sifted)'
  - '1/4 cup Crisco'
---

## Directions

1. Grease pan. Get small handful of Crisco (approximately (¼ cup). Add a little buttermilk. Mix with hand in flour. Continue adding buttermilk – a little at a time. Add a little more flour if needed.). Roll into biscuits. Put on greased cookie sheet and flatten with hand.
2. Bake at 425° for 10 minutes. If not brown, broil for a minute or two watching to make sure don’t burn.

## Notes

If not brown after 10 minutes, turn to broil and watch till brown on top.
