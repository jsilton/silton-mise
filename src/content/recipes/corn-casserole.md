---
title: 'Corn Casserole'
ingredients:
  - '1/4 lb butter, melted (1 stick)'
  - '2 eggs, well beaten'
  - '8 oz sour cream'
  - '8 oz cream corn'
  - '8 oz whole corn, drained'
  - '1 box Jiffy Corn Bread Mix'
---

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Put in casserole dish.
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes.
