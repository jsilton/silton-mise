---
title: 'Kale Salad with Mango, Avocado and Feta'
ingredients:
  - '1 head of kale ribs removed, chopped finely'
  - '1 mango cubed'
  - '1/2 purple onion diced'
  - '1/2 cup almonds, pepitas, or sunflower seeds, lightly toasted'
  - '1 large avocado diced'
  - '1 cup feta cheese crumbled'
  - 'Dressing'
  - '1 orange, zest and juice'
  - '1 garlic clove minced'
  - '1 teaspoon Dijon mustard'
  - '2 teaspoons honey'
  - '1/3 cup olive oil, plus extra for massaging'
  - 'Salt and pepper'
---

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, drizzle kale with 2 or 3 tablespoons of olive oil and a 1/4 teaspoon salt and massage the leaves for a couple of minutes to soften them.
2. Add, mango, feta, onion and pepitas.
3. To make dressing, whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl. Toss with salad.
4. Add the avocado last, so it doesn't get smashed and adjust salt and pepper if necessary.
