## Directions

1. 1. Whisk vinegar and honey together in a small bowl. Add the sliced strawberries and mix to coat. Let sit for 30-minutes.
2. 2. Place all the ingredients in blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Makes about 1/2 cup.
