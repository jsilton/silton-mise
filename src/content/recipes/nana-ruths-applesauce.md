## Directions

1. Core and peel apples, cut into chunks.
2. Add apple chunks to nonstick (Le Creuset) pot. Fill with water and rinse. Fill with water and rinse second time leaving 1/4 water.
3. Squeeze less than 3/4 lemon, add honey, sugar and spices.
4. Cook on high until it boils, then cover and reduce heat to very low.
5. Cook on very low for 20-25 minutes, mixing often.
6. When soft, crush apples against side of pot or with masher. Cook down.

## Notes

Traditional for Rosh Hashanah, Thanksgiving and Hanukkah
