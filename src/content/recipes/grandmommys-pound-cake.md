---
title: 'Grandmommy''s Pound Cake'
ingredients:
  - '1/2 lb butter'
  - '1 stick margarine, soft'
  - '6 eggs, room temperature'
  - '2 cups sugar'
  - '3 cups, pre-sifted flour'
  - '1 tsp lemon flavor'
  - '1 tsp almond flavor'
  - '1 tsp vanilla'
---

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar well.
2. Alternate adding flour and eggs.
3. Add flavor last.
4. Grease and flour pan.
5. For large cake, bake at 275 degrees for 1.5 hours.
6. For 3 loaves, bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour.
