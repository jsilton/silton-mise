---
title: Grilled Chicken with Herbed Corn Salsa
servings: 'Yield: 4 servings'
ingredients:
  - '2 1/4 lb. skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs'
  - salt
  - pepper
  - '2 large ears corn, shucked'
  - 1 c. packed fresh basil leaves
  - 1/2 c. packed fresh mint leaves
  - 1/4 c. Grated Parmesan
  - 1 clove garlic
  - 1/4 c. lemon juice
  - 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
---

## Directions

1. Season chicken thighs with 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Grill chicken on medium, covered, 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked (165 degrees F), turning over once.
2. Grill corn on medium 10 minutes, turning occasionally. In food processor, pulse basil, mint, Parmesan, garlic, and 1/4 teaspoon salt until finely chopped. Add lemon juice and olive oil; pulse until just combined. Cut kernels off cobs; stir into herb sauce. Serve chicken with corn mixture.
