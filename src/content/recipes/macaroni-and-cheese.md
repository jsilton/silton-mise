---
title: 'Macaroni and Cheese'
ingredients:
  - '8 oz. macaroni'
  - '1 lb Cheese (medium) cut in chunks'
  - '4 eggs'
  - '1/2 stick margarine'
  - 'milk'
  - '1/2-3/4 tsp salt'
---

## Directions

1. Boil macaroni in salted water for 10 minutes. Drain.
2. Cut cheese and add to macaroni.
3. Add thin slices of margarine.
4. Add eggs.
5. Add milk untill almost completely covered.
6. Bake in 9x9 dish at 475 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
