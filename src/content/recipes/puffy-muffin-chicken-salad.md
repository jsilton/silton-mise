---
title: 'Puffy Muffin Chicken Salad'
ingredients:
  - '2 cups chicken tenders'
  - '1/2 cup celery, diced'
  - '2/3 cup grapes, quartered'
  - '1/2 cup mayonnaise'
  - 'Salt and ground black pepper'
---

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken tenders in water until done. Drain and allow to cool.
2. Pull, dont cut, the chicken into pieces in a mixing bowl. Add the celery, grapes, salt and pepper. Mix slightly.
3. Stir in the mayonnaise with a fork or spoon. Adjust the flavor by adding more salt and pepper, abd adjust the consistency by adding more mayonnaise.
