---
title: 'ChatGTP the Park'
ingredients:
  - '4.5 oz Hendrics gin'
  - '1 can Spindrift Grapefruit (about 12 oz, to top)'
  - '2 oz fresh lime juice'
  - '0.5 oz agave syrup (for just a hint of sweetness)'
  - '6 dashes grapefruit bitters (2 per glass)'
  - 'Lime wheels or grapefruit slices for garnish'
---

Makes 3 servings

## Directions

1. In a shaker, combine gin, lime juice, and agave syrup (if using) with ice. Shake well.
2. Strain into ice-filled glasses, dividing evenly.
3. Top each glass with Spindrift Grapefruit.
4. Add 2 dashes of grapefruit bitters to each glass and stir gently.
5. Garnish with lime wheels or grapefruit slices.
