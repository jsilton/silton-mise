---
title: 'Grandmommy''s Pecan Pie'
servings: '2 pies'
ingredients:
  - '1/2 stick margarine, melted'
  - '1 1/4 cups sugar'
  - '1/2 cup Karo (white)'
  - '4 eggs, slightly beaten'
  - '1 tsp vanilla'
  - '2 cups of pecans'
  - '2 pie shells'
---

## Directions

1. Mix in this order:1. sugar2. Karo3. eggs4. margarine5. vanilla6. nuts
2. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes in 2 pie shells.
