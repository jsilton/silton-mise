---
title: 'In the Park'
ingredients:
  - '5 Basil leaves'
  - '1/2 Lemon cut into 4 pieces'
  - '3/4 oz Agave Nectar'
  - '2 oz Fresh Squeezed Grapefruit Juice'
  - '2 oz Gin'
  - '3 dash Grapefruit Bitters'
---

## Directions

1. Layer 1st three ingredients in glass in the above order and muddle gently. Add Gin, juice, bitters and ice to the shaker, and shake vigorously.
2. Double strain over ice in a highball glass. Garnish with Freshly spanked basil leaf.
