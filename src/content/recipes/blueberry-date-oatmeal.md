---
title: Blueberry Date Oatmeal
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 7 min
servings: '2'
ingredients:
  - 1 cup rolled oats
  - 2 Tbs frozen riced cauliflower
  - >-
    1 1/2 cup water (can substitute all or part milk/milk substitute, or add
    more) 6 dates, chopped
  - 2 Tbs coconut oil
  - 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
totalTime: 17 min
---

## Directions

1. 1. Mix oats, cauliflower, water, dates, and oatmeal in a bowl.2. Microwave on high for approximately 6 minutes. Alternately, cook on stovetop, stirring frequently.3. Stir in blueberries before serving.
