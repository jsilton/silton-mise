---
title: 'Sweet & White Potato Gratin'
ingredients:
  - '2 lbs of sweet potatoes'
  - '1 1/2 lbs of white potatoes'
  - '2 1/2 cup shredded gruyere cheese'
  - '2 cup finely grated parmesan reggiano cheese'
  - '3 cup heavy cream'
  - '2 tsp salt'
  - '1 tsp pepper'
  - '3 cloves minced garlic'
  - '1 tbsp fresh chopped thyme'
  - '1 tbsp fresh chopped sage'
  - '1 tsp fresh chopped rosemary'
  - '1/4 tsp nutmeg'
  - 'Dash of cayenne pepper'
---

## Directions

1. Whisk 3 c heavy cream, 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 3 cloves minced garlic, 1 tbsp fresh chopped thyme, 1 tbsp fresh chopped sage, 1 tsp fresh chopped rosemary, 1/4 tsp nutmeg & a dash of cayenne pepper in a large bowl
2. In a separate bowl mix 2 1/2 c shredded gruyere cheese & 2 c finely grated parmesan reggiano cheese.
3. Peel 2 lbs of sweet potatoes & 1 1/2 lbs of white potatoes.
4. Carefully use a handheld mandolin slicer and slice peeled potatoes into thin rounds.
5. Pour 1/2 c of the cream into the bottom of a casserole dish then neatly layer one layer of the sweet potatoes having them overlap slightly.
6. Drizzle 3/4 c of cream mixture on top & sprinkle 1 c of cheese on top of that.
7. Next layer the white potatoes and repeat the steps, then a sweet potato layer repeating the steps.
8. For the top layer of potatoes layer with both colors & sprinkle all remaining cheese on top and garnish with chopped thyme.
9. Cover with foil & bake on 425 for 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
10. Remove foil & reduce heat to 400 degrees & bake for 45 minutes until top is golden & bubbly.
11. Let cool & serve as a Thanksgiving side dish, then EAT!
