---
title: 'Chicken Fingers'
servings: '1'
ingredients:
  - '2 tsp butter'
  - 'Ritz crackers, crushed'
  - '1-1.5 chicken strips'
---

## Directions

1. Melt butter, then coat chicken strips in butter.
2. Put crushed Ritz crackers in a large baggie, then drop in chicken strips and shake to coat.
3. Put chicken strips on a baking sheet covered with foil. Bake at 425 degrees uncovered for 15 minutes.
4. Turn chicken strips and bake 10-15 minutes or until no longer pink in center.
