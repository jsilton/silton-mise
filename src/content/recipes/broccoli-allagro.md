---
title: Broccoli all'Agro
prepTime: 5 min
cookTime: 5 min
servings: '6'
ingredients:
  - 2 large heads Broccoli (florets only)
  - 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  - 1 Lemon (Juice and Zest)
  - 1 small clove Garlic (grated into paste)
  - 1/2 tsp Salt
totalTime: 10 min
---

Steamed broccoli usually tastes watery. This method fixes that by tossing the broccoli in a "Citronette" (Lemon-Garlic oil) while it is hot and dry, allowing the flavor to absorb into the floret like pasta.

## Directions

1. The Dressing: While water boils, whisk the olive oil, lemon juice, zest, grated garlic, and salt in the bottom of your serving bowl.
2. Steam: Steam broccoli for 4â€“5 minutes (until tender-crisp).
3. Dry: Remove from steamer and shake off ALL excess water. (Water blocks flavor).
4. Toss: Dump the hot, dry broccoli directly into the serving bowl with the dressing. Toss vigorously. The heat creates a fragrant garlic-lemon sauce that clings to the veg.
