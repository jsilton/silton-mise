servings: '6'
ingredients:
  - '1 large butternut squash (about 3-4 pounds) cored, peeled and quartered'
  - '1/2 cup unsalted butter, but into pieces, room temperature'
  - '1/4 cup sugar (gradually to taste)'
  - '1/8 cup pure maple syrup'
  - '1/2 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg (can be bottled nutmeg)'
  - '1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper'
---

## Directions

1. Bring large pot of water to boil. Add squash and cook until easily pierced with knife/fork, about 25 minutes.
2. Drain, then transfer to heavy large saucepan (can be same pot).
3. Coarsely mash squash using potato masher.
4. Mix in remaining ingredients.
5. Stir over low heat to heat through.
6. Taste and adjust seasoning.

## Notes

6 servings (doubles and triples easily)
