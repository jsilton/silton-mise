---
title: Green Smoothie for Kids with 2 Hidden Veggies!
prepTime: 5 min
cookTime: 5 min
ingredients:
  - ¾ cup Almond Milk - or milk of choice
  - 1 small/medium Banana
  - ¼ ripe Avocado - no black/brown spots on its skin
  - ½ cup Frozen Mango Chunks
  - 1 tablespoon Rolled Oats
  - 1 cup Baby Spinach
  - '2 teaspoons Nut Butter - peanut butter, sunflower seed butter'
  - ½ teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  - Optional – adjust sweetness
  - 1-2 teaspoons Maple Syrup - or 1-2 drops natural stevia
totalTime: 10 min
---

## Directions

1. In the jug of a high-speed blender, add all the ingredients above in the order listed.
2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust thickness and sweetness. For a frothier smoothie, add few ice cubes. For a thin smoothie, add a splash of almond milk. For sweetness, add a few drops of stevia or 1-2 teaspoons of maple syrup. Blend after each adjustment and serve immediately.

## Notes

Gluten-free option: replace rolled oats with a gluten-free cereal like millet flakes.
