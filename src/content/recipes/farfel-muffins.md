---
title: Farfel Muffins
prepTime: 15 min
cookTime: 20 min
servings: 8 muffins
ingredients:
  - 3 cups matzo farfel
  - 2 cups boiling water
  - '5 tbsp butter, melted'
  - 4 eggs
  - Kosher salt
totalTime: 35 min
---

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Mix together matzo farfel and boiling water, then cool.
3. Add butter then eggs, one at a time, and salt to taste.
4. Pour into well-greased muffin tin and bake 20 minutes until golden.

## Notes

Doubles well. Best served warm.
