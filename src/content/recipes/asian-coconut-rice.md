---
title: Asian Coconut Rice
prepTime: 5 min
cookTime: 20 min
servings: '4'
ingredients:
  - 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  - 1 1/4 cups water
  - 1 teaspoon sugar
  - 1 pinch salt
  - 1 1/2 cups uncooked jasmine rice
totalTime: 25 min
---

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, water, sugar, and salt. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Stir in rice. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 18 to 20 minutes, until rice is tender. Watch Now
