---
title: 'Strawberry Congealed Salad'
ingredients:
  - '1 large package strawberry Jello'
  - '1 cup boiling water'
  - '2 10oz packages frozen strawberries, thawed and drained'
  - '1 large can crushed pineapple, drained'
  - '3 medium bananas, mashed'
  - '1 cup copped pecans, optional'
  - '1 pint sour cream'
---

## Directions

1. Mix Jello and boiling water until Jello dissolves.
2. Add thawed strawberries, pineapple, bans, and pecans (optional).
3. Mix all ingredients together.
4. Put half the mixture in long dish and let congeal (1-2 hours).
5. Spread sour cream on the congealed half. Pour rest of the Jello mixture on top of sour cream. Let congeal (1-2 hours).
