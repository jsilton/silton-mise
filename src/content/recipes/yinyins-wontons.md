---
title: 'YinYin''s Wontons'
ingredients:
  - '3-4 oz mushrooms, finely chopped'
  - '1/4 lb shrimp'
  - '1 lb gound lean pork'
  - '2-3 stalks green onion, finely chopped'
  - '1 (8 oz) can sliced water chestnuts, finely chopper'
  - '1-2 tbsp garlic salt'
  - '1 tbsp ginger, freshly grated'
  - '1 dash white (or black) pepper'
  - '1 tbsp dry sherry'
  - '1 tbsp soy sauce'
  - '1 egg'
  - '2 dash cilantro (if available)'
---

## Directions

1. Mix it all up.
2. Test two little tabs in microwave (30 seconds).
3. Test for salt (add more garlic salt or soy sauce if needed).
4. Roll into wonton skins wetting half of skin to stick.
