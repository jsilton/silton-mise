## Meal Overview

When you need dinner fast, this shrimp stir-fry delivers. The garlic-honey-lime sauce is addictive, and shrimp cook in minutes. Start to table in 30 minutes makes this a go-to for busy weeknights.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start rice immediately** - it's the longest component
2. **Prep shrimp** - peel, devein if needed, pat dry
3. **Make sauce** - whisk garlic, honey, lime, soy
4. **Steam broccoli** while rice finishes
5. **Cook shrimp** - 2-3 minutes per side, don't overcook
6. **Add sauce** - coat shrimp, let glaze thicken
7. **Serve over rice** with broccoli alongside

## Notes

- Use large shrimp (16-20 count) for easier cooking
- The sauce also works great on chicken or tofu
- Add red pepper flakes for heat
