Coq au Vin Slow Cooker v2

★★★★★

Dinner, Slow Cooker

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 3 hours Servings: 6

Ingredients

2 lbs Chicken Thighs (boneless, skinless)

16 oz Baby Bella mushrooms (approx. two 8oz containers), stems removed, wiped clean, sliced thick

6 Carrots, peeled and chopped into 1-inch chunks

10 oz Frozen Pearl Onions (1 bag)

4 cloves Garlic, minced

1 packet Beefy Onion Soup Mix

1/2 cup Dry Red Wine

1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce

1 tsp Cornstarch

1 tsp Dried Thyme

Garlic Powder (generous amount)

Pepper

Cooking Spray

Rice (for serving)

Directions

Brown the Chicken: Season chicken thighs with pepper. Brown them in a skillet on both sides (about 3-4 minutes total).

Prepare Veggie Base: Spray the crockpot with cooking spray. Add the minced garlic, carrot chunks, frozen pearl onions, and the thick-sliced, de-stemmed mushrooms.

Stack: Place the browned chicken thighs on top of the vegetables.

Make the Slurry: In a small cup, whisk the cornstarch into the red wine until it is completely dissolved (no lumps). Stir in the Worcestershire sauce and dried thyme.

Add Liquid: Pour the wine/cornstarch mixture directly over the chicken.

Season: Sprinkle the Beefy Onion Soup Mix and a generous amount of Garlic Powder over the top.

Cook: Cover and cook on High for 2 ½ – 3 hours.

Serve: Serve hot over rice.

Notes

Mushrooms: Use Baby Bellas (Cremini) for meatier texture; slice thick so they don't dissolve.

Onions: Frozen pearl onions are used for better texture and zero prep. No need to thaw before adding.  

Thickener: The cornstarch is whisked into the cold wine to prevent lumps.

Nutrition

Calories: 285 kcal
Protein: 29g
Carbs: 16g
Fat: 9g
Sodium: ~550mg (varies by soup mix brand)

Note: Adding 1 cup of cooked white rice adds approx. 200 calories and 45g carbs.
