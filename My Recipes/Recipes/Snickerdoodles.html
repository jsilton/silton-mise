Snickerdoodles

Dessert

Difficulty: Easy Servings: Makes 70 or more Source: Mimi (Pat Hamilton)

Ingredients

1 cup margarine or butter

1 1/2 cups sugar

2 eggs

2 3/4 cups pre-sifted flour

2 tsp cream of tarter

1 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp salt


Topping

3 tbsp sugar

3 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions

Mix butter, sugar and eggs.

Add flour, cream of tarter, soda and salt and stir into mixture.

Chill in refrigerator.

Roll into walnut size balls

Roll each ball in a mixture of sugar and cinnamon.

Bake 8 minutes at 400 degrees, spacing balls on cookie sheet to allow room for spreading.
