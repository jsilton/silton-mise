Chicken Coq au Vin

Dinner, Slow Cooker

Prep Time: 10 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

1.5 lb chicken thighs

4 cloves garlic

6 carrots chopped

2 package of mushrooms

1 onion chopped

beefy onion soup mix

1/2 cup dry red wine

garlic salt

pepper

cooking spray

Rice

Directions

Brown chicken thighs on both sides
Spray crockpot with cooking spray
Add minced garlic, chopped carrots, mushrooms and onion
Add chicken and pour beefy onion soup mix and red wine over chicken
Season with garlic salt and pepper
Cook on high for 2 1/2-3 hours
