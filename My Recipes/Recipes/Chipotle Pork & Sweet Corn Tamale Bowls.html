Chipotle Pork & Sweet Corn "Tamale" Bowls

★★★★★

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 20 Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

The "Masa" Base (Polenta):

1 cup Dry Polenta (Cornmeal)

4 cups Chicken Stock

2 tbsp Butter

1 cup Frozen Corn

1/2 cup Mexican Shredded Cheese

1/2 tsp Cumin


Smoky Pork Filling:

1.5 lbs Ground Pork

1 Red Onion, diced

1 Red Bell Pepper, diced

3 cloves Garlic, minced

2-3 Chipotle Peppers in Adobo, minced + 1 tbsp sauce

1 can (14.5 oz) Fire Roasted Crushed Tomatoes

1 tsp Dried Oregano

Salt & Pepper


Toppings:

1 Avocado (cubed) tossed

1/2 Lime (juiced)

pinch of salt

Radishes, sliced paper thin

Tortilla Chips, crushed

Greek Yogurt

Directions

The Base (Polenta): Bring 4 cups Chicken Stock to a boil. Whisk in Polenta and 1/2 tsp Cumin. Reduce heat to low. Simmer 15 mins.

The Chef Move: When polenta is thickened, stir in the Frozen Corn, Butter, and Cheese. The corn will cook in the residual heat of the mush.

The Sear: While polenta bubbles, heat oil in a large skillet on high. Add Pork, Red Onion, and Bell Pepper. Cook aggressively—don't stir too often—until pork is browned and peppers have char marks (approx 8 mins). Drain excess grease if it's pooling.

The Bloom: Push meat to the side. Add minced Garlic, Oregano, and Chipotle Peppers. Fry them in the pan center for 45 seconds until aromatic.

The Sauce: Pour in Fire Roasted Tomatoes. Scrape up the browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.

Assembly: Ladle Sweet Corn Polenta into bowls. Top with Smoky Pork. Crown with the Avocado Lime mix, Radishes, Yogurt, and Crushed Chips.

Nutrition

Calories: ~610 kcal
Protein: 33g
Carbs: 42g
