Banana Nut Bread

Source: Hamilton Family

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter or margarine

1 cup sugar

2 eggs

3 large very ripe bananas, mashed

2 cups sifted all purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup chopped nuts, optional

Directions

Cream butter and sugar thoroughly.

Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.

Add mashed bands and beat well.

Add flour, soda and salt to butter-sugar-banana mixture, mixing well.

Stir in nuts (optional).

Pour into greased and floured 9x5x3 loaf pan.

Bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes.

Allow to cool.

Notes

For two small loafs, bake for 40 minutes.
