Patate al Forno

★★★★★

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 40 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 6

Ingredients

2 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes (peeled and cut into large chunks)

3 tbsp Olive Oil

1 tsp Salt

1/2 tsp Garlic Powder (Fresh garlic burns at this heat)

2 sprigs Fresh Rosemary (chopped)

Description

The definitive Roman-style roasted potato. The extra step of par-boiling creates a rough, starchy exterior that shatters into a glass-like crunch when roasted, while the inside stays creamy.

Directions

Par-Boil: Preheat oven to 425°F. Place potato chunks in a pot of cold salted water. Boil for 7 minutes. (Outside should be soft, inside raw).
Rough Up: Drain water. Shake the potatoes vigorously in the colander to "bruise" the edges. (This fuzz becomes the crunch).
Season: Toss the hot potatoes in a bowl with olive oil, salt, garlic powder, and rosemary.
Roast: Spread on a baking sheet (ensure they don't touch). Roast for 35 minutes, flipping once halfway through.
Serve: Serve immediately while hot and crispy.
