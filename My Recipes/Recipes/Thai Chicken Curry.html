Thai Chicken Curry

Dinner, Thai

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Jordan Silton

Ingredients

1 lb chicken breasts, thinly-sliced

1 red pepper, cut into long strips

1 green pepper, cut into long strips

1 large yellow oniom, cut into long strips

2-4 tbsp red curry paste, to taste

1 can coconut milk

1 tbsp brown sugar

2 tbsp butter

1 tbsp cajun spice

1/3 cup fresh thai basil, chopped

1.5 cups Jasmine rice, cooked in a rice cooker

Directions

Combine the coconut milk, brown sugar, cajun spice and curry paste in a small bowl. Whisk until disolved to form a sauce.

Bring butter to a medium heat in a wok. Add sauce bring to an active simmer for 3 minutes.

Add the chicken, peppers and onions, and return to an active simmer for 15 minutes.

Add the basil and serve over jasmine rice.
