Chocolate Bread Pudding with Vanilla Ice Cream

Prep Time: 25 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Total Time: 45 minutes Difficulty: Medium Servings: 8 Source: The Ritz-Carlton, Cancun

Ingredients

13 cups brioche, 3/4 inch cubes

6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped

6 ounces white chocolate chips

4 large eggs

1/2 cup sugar

4 tablespoons sugar

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1 1/2 cups heavy cream

1/2 cup whole milk

2 cups vanilla bean ice cream

Directions

Preheat oen to 350 degens.

Combine bread, chocolate, and white chocolate in a large bowl (toss to blend).

In mixer, beat eggs, sugar and vanilla extract.

Gradually beat in heavy cream and milk. Add cream to the bread mixture (stir to combine). Let stand 15 minutes.

Spray 8 (6oz) scuffle cups with non-stick spray. Transfer bread pudding to prepared dishes, spreading evenly.

Drizzle with remaining 1/2 cup cream and sugar on bread pudding.

Bake until edges are golden and custard is set in center (15 minutes). When done, serve with vanilla ice cream.

Notes

Bread pudding mixture can be made up to 12 hours ahead of time.
