Salsa Verde Chicken Casserole

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: prep/total time: 30 min. Servings: 6 servings Source: Myfussyeater.com

Ingredients

2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken

1 cup (8 ounces) sour cream

1-1/2 cups salsa verde, divided

8 corn tortillas (6 inches)

2 cups chopped tomatoes

1/4 cup minced fresh cilantro

2 cups (8 ounces) shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Optional toppings: avocado slices, thinly sliced green onions or fresh cilantro leaves

Nutrition

400 calories: 1 serving (calculated without optional toppings)
Total Carbohydrates: 22g carbohydrate (5g sugars 102mg cholesterol 23g fat (13g saturated fat)
3g fiber)
26g protein.
637mg sodium
