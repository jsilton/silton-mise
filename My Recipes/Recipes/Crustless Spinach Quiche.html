Crustless Spinach Quiche

Quiche

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 30 min Servings: Serves: 6-8 Source: Rebecca Cybulsky

Ingredients

1 yellow onion

10 oz spinach

6 eggs

gruyere cheese

Directions

Preheat oven to 350-degrees F.

Blacken onion. Shred cheese. Saute spinach.

Spray 9-inch pie plate with Cooking spray.

Mix all ingredients throughly.

Turn into pie pan, spreading top evenly.

Bake until top is browned, 30 minutes.
