Pad See Ew

Dinner, Thai

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2

Ingredients

1 lb Chinese broccoli

1 lb fresh flat rice noodles

1/2 cup thinly sliced pork or beef

2 cloves fresh garlic

1 tbsp dark soy sauce

1 tbsp light soy sauce

1 tbsp sugar

2 tbsp vegetable oil

1 egg

Directions

If your rice noodles are not pre-cut, cut them into strips of 3/4 inch wide. if your noodles came out from the refrigerator, heat them up in the microwave first.

Cut Chinese broccoli into 2 inch long piles. Halve the stems lengthwise because thick stems take longer to cook.

Heat wok to high heat and then add vegetable oil. Drop in the copped garlic and stir.

Add the slices pork or beef. Stir to cook.

When the pork or beef is somewhat cooker or turned from pink to light brown, add rice noodles. Stir to break up the noodles.

Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and sugar. Stir to mix the seasonings.

Open a spot in the middle of the pan, and drop the egg in. Scramble the egg until it is almost all cooked, then fold in the noodles and mix them all.

Add the Chinese broccoli, stems first. As soon as the Chinese broccoli is cooked, turn off the heat.
