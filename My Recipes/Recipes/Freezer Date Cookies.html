Freezer Date Cookies

Kids Eat in Color

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 15 min Total Time: 30 min Servings: 24 cookies

Ingredients

1 cup medjool dates (can use other types of dates if soaked in hot water first), pits removed

1/2 cup butter, melted

1 large egg

1 tsp vanilla

1/3 cup buckwheat flour

2/3 cup white whole wheat flour

1 cup quick oats

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp cinnamon

1/4 cup plain greek yogurt

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F/175 degrees C.
2. Put dates and butter in a food processor. Process until smooth.
3. Add egg, yogurt and vanilla to the food processor. Mix thoroughly.
4. Mix buckwheat flour, white whole wheat flour, oats, baking soda, and cinnamon in a bowl.
5. Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients and mix fully.
6. Using your hands, make small balls of dough. Place on
greased cookie sheets and press down on the top.
7. Bake 12-15 min - until lightly brown.
