Sweet & White Potato Gratin

Thanksgiving

Difficulty: Medium Source: Kaitlin Gwock

Ingredients

2 lbs of sweet potatoes

1 1/2 lbs of white potatoes

2 1/2 cup shredded gruyere cheese

2 cup finely grated parmesan reggiano cheese

3 cup heavy cream

2 tsp salt

1 tsp pepper

3 cloves minced garlic

1 tbsp fresh chopped thyme

1 tbsp fresh chopped sage

1 tsp fresh chopped rosemary

1/4 tsp nutmeg

Dash of cayenne pepper


Directions

Whisk 3 c heavy cream, 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 3 cloves minced garlic, 1 tbsp fresh chopped thyme, 1 tbsp fresh chopped sage, 1 tsp fresh chopped rosemary, 1/4 tsp nutmeg & a dash of cayenne pepper in a large bowl

In a separate bowl mix 2 1/2 c shredded gruyere cheese & 2 c finely grated parmesan reggiano cheese.

Peel 2 lbs of sweet potatoes & 1 1/2 lbs of white potatoes.

Carefully use a handheld mandolin slicer and slice peeled potatoes into thin rounds.

Pour 1/2 c of the cream into the bottom of a casserole dish then neatly layer one layer of the sweet potatoes having them overlap slightly.

Drizzle 3/4 c of cream mixture on top & sprinkle 1 c of cheese on top of that.

Next layer the white potatoes and repeat the steps, then a sweet potato layer repeating the steps.

For the top layer of potatoes layer with both colors & sprinkle all remaining cheese on top and garnish with chopped thyme.

Cover with foil & bake on 425 for 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.

Remove foil & reduce heat to 400 degrees & bake for 45 minutes until top is golden & bubbly.

Let cool & serve as a Thanksgiving side dish, then EAT!
