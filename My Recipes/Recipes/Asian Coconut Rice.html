Asian Coconut Rice

Prep Time: 5 m Cook Time: 20 m Servings: 4 Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk

1 1/4 cups water

1 teaspoon sugar

1 pinch salt

1 1/2 cups uncooked jasmine rice

Directions

In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, water, sugar, and salt. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Stir in rice. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 18 to 20 minutes, until rice is tender. Watch Now
