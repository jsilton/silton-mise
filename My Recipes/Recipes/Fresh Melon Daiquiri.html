Fresh Melon Daiquiri

Drinks

Prep Time: 5 minutes Total Time: 5 minutes Servings: 4 Source: The Ritz-Carlton, Cancun

Ingredients

6 cups honeydew melon, peeled, deseeded and cubed

1 cup light rum

Directions
