Garlic Mashed Red Potatoes

Sides

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: prep/total time: 30 min. Servings: 6 servings Source: tasteofhome.com

Ingredients

8 medium red potatoes, quartered

3 garlic cloves, peeled

2 tablespoons butter

1/2 cup fat-free milk, warmed

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

Place potatoes and garlic in a large saucepan; cover with water.

Bring to a boil.

Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are very tender.

Drain well.

Add the butter, milk and salt; mash.

Stir in cheese.
