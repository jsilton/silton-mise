Jeri's Lentil Soup

Slow Cooker, Soup

Servings: 12 cups Source: Pat Miller

Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil

2 cups chopped onion

3 carrots, thinly-sliced into copper penies

1 tsp Italian seasoning (or 3/4 tsp each dried thyme abd marjoram)

28 oz can low salt crushed tomatoes

56 oz (7 cups) low fat chicken broth (or vegetable broth)

16 oz dry lentils, washed and drained

1/4 tsp ground black pepper or more to taste

6 oz white wine (Pinot Grigio)

3 tbsp dried parsley

Directions

This single batch recipe makes 12 cups. I generally make double and freeze as 6 packets, (2 cups jn each quart bag to make a dinner seving for two).

Heat oil and add onions and carrots in a large frying pan and saute for 5 minutes. Add marjoram and thyme or Italian seasoning to the vegetables as they cook. Transfer to a slow cooker when translucent.

Put tomatoes in slow cooker and add the seasoned onions and carrots. Add broth and lentils. Cook in slow cooker on low for 5 hours.

Add wine, parsley and pepper and simmer for 30 minutes.
