Jewish, Sides

Source: Ruth Shankman

Ingredients

6 lbs McIntosh apples

1/4-1/2 cup Sugar

1/4 jar Honey

2-3 shakes Cinnamon

1 shake Nutmeg

1 lemon

Directions

Core and peel apples, cut into chunks.

Add apple chunks to nonstick (Le Creuset) pot. Fill with water and rinse. Fill with water and rinse second time leaving 1/4 water.

Squeeze less than 3/4 lemon, add honey, sugar and spices.

Cook on high until it boils, then cover and reduce heat to very low.

Cook on very low for 20-25 minutes, mixing often.

When soft, crush apples against side of pot or with masher. Cook down.

Notes

Traditional for Rosh Hashanah, Thanksgiving and Hanukkah
